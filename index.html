Inside the shelter

417 animals (110 dogs, 307 cats) are in our shelter, but with only a 20% adoption rate, most of them will be staying with us for the foreseeable future, hoping for a lucky break.

Our pets receive the very best: from grate food to sincere care for their emotional well-being. Behind every tail is a large team of 80 volunteers and 31 professionals. We love our work so much that one in eight pets (12%) goes home to live with our employees. Spending our days with our charges, we grow with them together, but their need to get home to you😉
